After this app was successfully developed and made available as "Proof of Concept" within the framework of the digitalswitzerland Challenge 2017 - 2019, it is now entering its next phase. With the aim of continued availability to users and at the same time providing continued added value, dThx - Digital Therapeutics Ltd was launched as a startup, which will further develop the app and, in parallel, set up a blood pressure coach.

In order for you to stay on board, your consent to the "move" is required. The data protection and terms of use do not change with regard to content. Nevertheless, your consent is required for the continued storage and analysis of your data in the new company environment. The concrete details can be viewed at https://www.dthx.ch/legal/privacy/en & https://www.dthx.ch/legal/terms/en.

With your consent, the use of the app and the newly added features is also guaranteed. If you do not agree to the "move", your account, including all data recorded, will be irrevocably deleted on 30.11.2019 or, if you so wish, at any time previous. Nothing stands in the way of a later new registration.
